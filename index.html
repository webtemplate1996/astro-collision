Know the signs

Do You Need Suspension Repair?

Suspension problems can affect comfort, control, braking, tire wear, and overall safety. If you notice any of these signs, schedule an inspection.

Car bounces too much after bumps
Clunking or knocking noises
Vehicle pulls to one side
Uneven tire wear
Steering feels loose or unstable
Front end dips when braking

What we repair

Suspension Services We Offer

Astro Collision helps diagnose and repair common suspension issues for drivers in Houston.

Suspension Inspection

We inspect key suspension components to find the cause of shaking, bouncing, noise, or poor ride quality.

Shock Replacement

Worn shocks can make your vehicle bounce, feel unstable, and reduce comfort on the road.

Strut Replacement

Bad struts can affect steering, braking stability, and how your vehicle handles bumps.

Ball Joint Replacement

Damaged ball joints may cause rattling, vibration, uneven tire wear, or unsafe steering behavior.

Sway Bar Service

Sway bar issues can create clunking sounds, poor handling, and extra body movement while turning.

Wheel & Tire Services

We can help with wheel-related issues, tire balancing, rotation, and related suspension concerns.

Suspension Repair FAQ

How do I know if my suspension is bad?

Common signs include bouncing, shaking, pulling to one side, clunking noises, uneven tire wear, or poor steering control.

Can I drive with suspension problems?

It depends on the damage, but suspension problems can affect safety, braking, steering, and tire wear. It is better to schedule an inspection.
