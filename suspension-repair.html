Warning signs

Signs You Need Suspension Repair

Suspension problems are not just uncomfortable. They can affect safety, steering, braking, and tire wear.

Car Bounces Too Much

If your vehicle keeps bouncing after bumps, your shocks or struts may be worn out.

Clunking Noises

Knocking or clunking sounds can come from damaged suspension components.

Uneven Tire Wear

Bad suspension can cause your tires to wear unevenly and reduce tire life.

Vehicle Pulls

If the vehicle pulls left or right, it may need inspection, alignment, or suspension repair.

Loose Steering

Unstable steering can be a sign of worn parts or suspension damage.

Front End Dips

If the front dips when braking, shocks or struts may need attention.
